Canada Fact Sheet

Introduction

History

Canada gained it's independence is 1867, while keeping an ally relationship with the UK. On the other hand, in 1982, Canada cut colonial ties with the UK and was no longer an entity under the UK. Economically and technologically, Canada's development was parallel to that of the US historically.
