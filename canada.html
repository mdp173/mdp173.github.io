Canada Fact Sheet

Introduction

History

Canada gained it's independence is 1867, while keeping an ally relationship with the UK. On the other hand, in 1982, Canada cut colonial ties with the UK and was no longer an entity under the UK. Economically and technologically, Canada's development was parallel to that of the US historically.

Political Challanges

Meeting public demands for quality improvements in:

  • Health Care
  • Education
  • Social Services
  • Economic Competitiveness

Responding to the particular concerns of:

  • Predominantly francophone Quebec

Canada also aims to develop its diverse energy resources while maintining its commitment to the environment.
